Oh Hi again, I'm Kavier!

I'm a UX Designer who is obsessed with turning ideas into desirable products. There’s nothing more exciting than synthesizing user research and crafting it into a solution that makes the user experience intuitive and enjoyable.

I'm a firm believer in constant learning and getting things done - to me, that’s what makes a great UX Designer; knowing when to be curious and when to be critical.

Outside of work, you can catch me shooting hoops, hitting the gym, hiking trails, or indulging in some good food. I also get creative designing logos as a fun side gig!

My motto: The best dream happens when you’re awake. So seize the day, embrace the opportunity for innovation, and make a difference in the way we experience the world.
